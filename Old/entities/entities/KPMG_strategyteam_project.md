## Project Scope
Market study into QSR (Quick Service Restaurant) and Casual Dining sectors in Vietnam focusing on:
- Market sizing and segmentation (5-10 year outlook)
- Competitive landscape evolution
- Consumer trends and behavior
- Risk assessment

## Key Research Questions

### Market Sizing & Segmentation
1. QSR and Casual Dining market development since 2015 vs overall restaurant sales
2. Foreign vs local brand performance and sizing
3. Investment case for foreign QSR brands - thesis vs reality
4. Growth drivers analysis (units, same-store sales, traffic)
5. Market saturation analysis across Tier 1, 2, 3 cities
6. JRG's addressable categories (fried chicken, pizza) vs alternatives (burgers, Chinese)
7. Consumer behavior trends and preferences by city tier
8. Sales channel breakdown (dine-in, delivery, takeaway, aggregator)
9. 10-year outlook for QSR, Casual Dining, and JRG's categories
10. Key risks to base case (health consciousness, alternative categories)

### Competitive Landscape
1. Market share breakdown by major competitors
2. Share trends since 2015 and leading players' strategies
3. Foreign operators' store footprint evolution
4. Winners and losers among foreign QSR operators (past 10 years)
5. Success drivers and sustainable competitive moats
6. Store network optimization strategies
7. JRG's competitive positioning and market-beating potential
8. Brand loyalty building strategies
9. Future competitive landscape evolution and key battlegrounds
10. Competitor responses to emerging trends
11. Food aggregator outlook and competitive advantages
12. New foreign entrant visibility and investment cases

### Profitability & Operations
1. Market leaders in profitability and drivers
2. Indicative margin profiles (store-level, market-level)
3. Macro/regulatory factors impacting profitability (inflation, labor, rental)
4. Cost structure analysis (COS, COL, Royalties, AMP, Premises %)
5. Responses to rental inflation and optimal store location/size shifts
6. FX and raw material volatility impacts
7. Requirements to "win" in Vietnamese market
8. Benchmark operators (Jollibee's, McDonald's, Pizza 4P's)
9. Gold standard foreign QSR operators and their success formula
