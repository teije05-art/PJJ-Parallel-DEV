# Medical Device Companies
## Overview
Medical device companies design, manufacture, and market medical devices intended for the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases and medical conditions.
## Examples of Medical Devices
- Implantable devices (e.g., pacemakers, artificial joints)
- Diagnostic equipment (e.g., MRI machines, ultrasound devices)
- Surgical instruments (e.g., scalpels, forceps)
- Medical imaging devices (e.g., X-ray machines, CT scanners)
