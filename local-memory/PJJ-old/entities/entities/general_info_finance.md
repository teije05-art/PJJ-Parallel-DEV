‚öôÔ∏è 3. How They Operate
* Intermediation Model: Bridge capital between those who have money (depositors/investors) and those who need it (borrowers/businesses).
* Revenue Sources:
   * Interest income (lending vs. deposit rates).
   * Fees & commissions (transactions, advisory, management).
   * Trading & investment gains.
   * Premiums (insurance).
* Regulation: Heavily regulated to ensure solvency, liquidity, and consumer protection (Basel III, AML/KYC laws).
* Risk Management: Use hedging, diversification, and capital buffers to manage credit, market, and operational risks.
* Technology Integration: Increasing reliance on digital banking, AI-based credit scoring, and blockchain.
