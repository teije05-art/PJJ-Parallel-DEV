
Risk Assessment Methodology
===========================
### Risk Identification
The risks associated with entering the Vietnamese retail food product market include regulatory risks, market risks, and operational risks.

### Risk Assessment
The risks will be assessed using a risk matrix, which will categorize the risks as high, medium, or low.

### Risk Mitigation
The risks will be mitigated by implementing a risk management plan, which will include strategies for reducing the likelihood and impact of the risks.
