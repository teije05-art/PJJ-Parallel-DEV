
Competitive Intelligence Analysis
===============================
### Competitor Profiling
The top competitors in the market are Vinmart, Big C, and Metro Cash & Carry. These companies have a strong presence in the market and offer a wide range of products.

### Competitor Strengths and Weaknesses
Vinmart is strong in terms of its brand reputation and customer loyalty, but weak in terms of its supply chain management. Big C is strong in terms of its pricing strategy, but weak in terms of its product quality. Metro Cash & Carry is strong in terms of its wholesale business, but weak in terms of its retail business.
