
# Cleveland Clinic Southeast Asia Market Entry Project

## Project Overview
- **Organization**: Cleveland Clinic - major US hospital group and healthcare provider
- **Strategic Goal**: Market entry into Southeast Asian healthcare market
- **Initial Focus Market**: Vietnam
- **Project Stage**: Planning phase
- **Primary Objectives**: 
  1. Develop solid operational base within Vietnam
  2. Identify and define target patient populations
  3. Establish strategic foundation for regional expansion

## Key Context from Healthcare Industry Knowledge
- Cleveland Clinic is a leading healthcare provider (similar to Mayo Clinic)
- Revenue model: Service fees and insurance reimbursements
- Operating in heavily regulated environment requiring government approvals
- Will need to navigate foreign direct investment regulations in Vietnam
- Partnership strategy critical for market entry
- Competition includes Raffles Medical and other international healthcare providers

## Strategic Framework Areas (from Japanese Hospital Vietnam framework)
1. Regulatory landscape and licensing requirements
2. Market positioning and competitive differentiation
3. Partnership strategy (joint ventures, government relations)
4. Localization requirements (cultural adaptation, staffing)
5. Location strategy (urban positioning, facility development)
6. Financial considerations (investment, pricing, reimbursement)
