# JP_RFP_Response_Requirements
## Introduction
The RFP response document must include:

1. **Executive Summary**: Compelling overview of our approach and why KPMG should be selected
2. **Understanding of Requirements**: Demonstrate deep comprehension of JP's needs for Vietnam QSR/Casual Dining market study
3. **Proposed Methodology**: Detailed research approach including:
   - Market sizing methodology (data sources, analytical frameworks)
   - Competitive landscape analysis approach
   - Consumer research methods (surveys, interviews, sample sizes)
   - Risk assessment framework
4. **Project Approach & Timeline**: 4-6 week delivery plan with milestones
5. **Team Composition**: Vietnam-based team with F&B expertise, relevant credentials
6. **Relevant Experience**: Past projects in QSR/Casual Dining and Vietnam F&B sector
7. **Deliverables**: Structured interview guides, case studies, interim/final reports, primary research data
8. **Pricing Structure**: Professional fees breakdown
9. **Why KPMG**: Differentiators and value proposition

This is a PROPOSAL DOCUMENT to win the project, not the execution of the project itself.
