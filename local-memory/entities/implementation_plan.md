## Detailed Implementation Plan
This plan outlines the steps required to implement the market entry strategy for the American healthcare company looking to do business in Vietnam.
## Phase 1: Market Research
Conduct a comprehensive market study into the QSR and Casual Dining sectors in Vietnam.
## Phase 2: Data Analysis
Analyze the market research data to understand market sizing and segmentation, competitive landscape evolution, and risk assessment.
## Phase 3: Report Development
Develop final reports on market study findings and recommendations.
