# Digital Transformation
## Project Description
The digital transformation project aims to leverage technology to improve business processes, enhance customer experience, and increase operational efficiency.

## Current Status
The project is currently in its planning phase, with the team identifying key areas for digital transformation and developing a roadmap for implementation.

## Methodologies and Requirements
The project will utilize a combination of agile methodologies, design thinking, and change management frameworks to ensure successful digital transformation. The team will also follow best practices for data governance, cybersecurity, and IT service management.
