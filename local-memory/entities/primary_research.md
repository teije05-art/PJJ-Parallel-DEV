## Primary Research Data
This document outlines the primary research data on consumer trends and behavior.
## Raw Data
The raw data collected from the surveys and interviews includes:
* Demographic information (age, gender, income level)
* Consumer preferences (favorite QSR brands, frequency of visit)
* Perceptions of QSR brands (quality, value, convenience)
## Analysis
The analysis of the primary research data reveals key trends and insights, including:
* The most popular QSR brands in Vietnam are KFC and Pizza Hut
* Consumers prioritize quality and value when it comes to QSR brands
* Convenience is a key factor in consumer choice when it comes to QSR brands
