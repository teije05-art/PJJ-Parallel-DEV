## Case Studies
This document outlines the case studies on major QSR brands in Vietnam.
## Case Study 1: KFC Vietnam
KFC Vietnam is one of the largest QSR chains in Vietnam, with over 100 locations across the country. The brand is known for its fried chicken and other American-style fast food.
## Case Study 2: Pizza Hut Vietnam
Pizza Hut Vietnam is another major QSR chain in Vietnam, with over 50 locations across the country. The brand is known for its pizza and other Italian-style fast food.
