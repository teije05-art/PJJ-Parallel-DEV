
Market Analysis Report

Introduction:
The market analysis report provides an overview of the QSR and Casual Dining sectors in Vietnam. The report includes market size, growth rate, competitive landscape, and consumer trends.

Market Size:
The QSR sector in Vietnam has a market size of approximately VND 10 trillion (USD 430 million) in 2022, with a growth rate of 10% per annum. The Casual Dining sector has a market size of approximately VND 5 trillion (USD 215 million) in 2022, with a growth rate of 12% per annum.

Competitive Landscape:
The QSR sector in Vietnam is dominated by international brands such as KFC, McDonald's, and Pizza Hut. The Casual Dining sector is dominated by local brands such as Pho 24 and Highlands Coffee.

Consumer Trends:
Consumers in Vietnam are increasingly health-conscious and are looking for healthier food options. There is also a growing demand for convenience and digital payment options.
